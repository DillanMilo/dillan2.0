Privacy notice

Last updated July 20, 2026

This notice explains what information is handled when you use the Dillan Milosevich portfolio website at www.dillanmilo.com.

Contact form

The contact form asks for your name, email address, and project message. That information is sent to Dillan so he can read and respond to your inquiry. The form is delivered through the site's hosting and email service providers.

Website analytics

The site uses Vercel Analytics and Google Analytics to understand page visits and interactions such as navigation, portfolio clicks, and contact form events. Those services may process device, browser, referral, and approximate location information according to their own policies. Contact form field contents are not intentionally included in analytics events.

External links

Portfolio and social links lead to independent websites. Their privacy practices are controlled by their respective owners.

Questions

Questions about this notice can be sent to dillan@creativecurrents.io.
